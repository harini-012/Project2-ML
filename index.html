Credit Card Customer Analysis

Input Details Explanation
  • Balance – Current amount available in the customer's account.
  • Purchases – Total amount spent using the credit card.
  • Cash Advance – Amount withdrawn as cash using the credit card.
  • Credit Limit – Maximum spending limit assigned to the customer.
  • Payments – Amount paid by the customer towards the credit card bill.

These financial attributes help the system understand the customer’s spending behaviour and determine their credit eligibility.

Enter Customer Financial Details
Decision Guidelines
  • The system analyzes customer spending behaviour using financial attributes.
  • A machine learning clustering model groups customers with similar financial patterns.
  • Based on the cluster category, the system determines:
    • Customer category
    • Credit card approval status
    • Recommended credit limit
    • Risk level
  • This helps banks make faster and data-driven credit approval decisions.
